Who We Are

Stratum Business Solution is a Tanzanian management consultancy specializing in regulatory advisory services across four key domains: TBS, TMDA, TRA, and BRELA.

Based in Mwenge, Kinondoni — Dar es Salaam, we serve individuals, startups, SMEs, and established enterprises seeking expert guidance through Tanzania's regulatory landscape. Our approach is personal, precise and always in the best interest of our clients.

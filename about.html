Who We Are

Built on Expertise.
Driven by Compliance.

Stratum Business Solution is a Tanzanian management consultancy registered with the Business Registrations and Licensing Agency (BRELA), specializing in regulatory advisory services across three critical domains: TMDA, TRA, and BRELA.

Based in Kijitonyama, Kinondoni — Dar es Salaam, we serve individuals, startups, SMEs, and established enterprises seeking expert guidance through Tanzania's regulatory landscape. Our approach is personal, precise and always in the best interest of our clients.

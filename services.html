Section 3.1

Consultancy in TBS Regulatory Affairs

We provide comprehensive consultancy on Tanzania Bureau of Standards (TBS) compliance requirements. Our team specializes in product registration for food and cosmetics, premise registration and inspections, ensuring your business meets all mandatory standards for market operations in Tanzania.

Product Registration: Mandatory for all pre-packaged food and cosmetics (including supplements) manufactured in or imported into Tanzania
Premise Registration: Business locations—including warehouses, retail shops, and manufacturing plants—must be inspected and registered
Standards Compliance Advisory: Guidance on product quality, labeling, packaging and hygiene standards required by TBS
Inspection Preparation and Support: Assistance in preparing for TBS inspections and addressing compliance findings
Documentation and Dossier Preparation: Complete preparation of required documentation for product and premise registration
Ongoing Compliance Monitoring: Continued support to maintain regulatory compliance and handle certificate renewals
TBS Advisory

Whether you're registering food products, cosmetics, or premises, TBS compliance is mandatory. Our specialists ensure smooth registration and ongoing adherence to Tanzania's quality standards.

Professional consultancy and advisory on all matters relating to TMDA regulations, standards and requirements
Assistance with registration, licensing, importation, exportation and distribution of medicines, medical devices and diagnostics
Preparation and submission of product dossiers, applications and all documentation required by TMDA
Advisory on compliance with TMDA regulations for health products including quality, safety and efficacy standards
Training and capacity building services on TMDA regulatory compliance for staff and management
Post-registration support and guidance on pharmacovigilance and adverse event reporting

We deliver expert consultancy and advisory services on all matters relating to the Tanzania Revenue Authority (TRA). From VAT registration to tax objection processes, our team provides end-to-end tax compliance support and represents clients directly in their dealings with TRA.

Consultancy and advisory services on tax compliance, tax planning and all TRA-related matters
Assistance with VAT registration, filing of tax returns and managing tax objection and appeal processes before TRA
Guidance on customs duties, excise duty, income tax, withholding tax and other levies administered by TRA
Client representation in all dealings, correspondence and negotiations with the Tanzania Revenue Authority
Tax audit support — preparation, documentation review and representation during TRA tax audits
Strategic tax planning to minimize liability and ensure ongoing compliance with Tanzanian tax law
